## Rating Guide

`[S]` -- Solved independently within interview time  
`[H]` -- Needed a hint but completed the code yourself  
`[L]` -- Looked at solution/editorial or could not code it cleanly  
`[R]` -- Redone successfully during revision

Rule:

Every `[H]` or `[L]` problem goes into the Revision Queue.

- Day 3 after first attempt
- Week 1 after first attempt
